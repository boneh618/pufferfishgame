# 🐡 Pufferfish Survival

A fun underwater survival game built in Java!  
Dodge enemies and spikes, puff up to defend yourself, and beat your high score.

[![Play Pufferfish Survival](https://img.shields.io/badge/🐡_Play_Now-In_Your_Browser!-blue?style=for-the-badge&logoColor=white)](https://boneh618.github.io/pufferfishgame/)

## How to Play

| Control | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **WASD / Arrow Keys** | Move |
| **SPACE** | Puff up (destroys enemies on contact) |
| **SHIFT** | Dash (quick burst of speed, destroys enemies) |
| **R** | Restart after game over |
| **ESC** | Quit |

## Features

- **Ocean environment** — gradient water, animated bubbles, light rays, swaying seaweed, sandy ocean floor
- **Power-ups** — collect shields, speed boosts, and extra lives
- **Combo system** — chain kills for score multipliers (up to x5)
- **3 enemy types** — normal, fast (blue), and big (dark red)
- **Particle effects** — explosions, damage flash, screen shake
- **Start menu & restart** — no more auto-closing after game over!
- **Dash ability** — quick dodge on SHIFT with cooldown
- **Invincibility frames** — brief protection after taking a hit
- **Persistent high score** — saved to `highscore.txt`
